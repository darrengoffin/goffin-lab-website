Research

Our lab uses a genetic, biochemical, and electrophysiological technologies to explore the pathogenesis and potential reversibility of the debilitating neurological disorder Rett Syndrome. Our research is supported by funding from the Medical Research Council (MRC) and benefits from being embedded within the Department of Biology and Department of Psychology, which houses expertise in biology, medicine and neuroscience.
